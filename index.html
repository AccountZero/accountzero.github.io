Javascript & Vue + (Ruby) on Rails

During my internship, I acquired proficiency in various programming languages that truly inspired me at the age of 19. Over time, I gained a deeper understanding of diverse concepts, significantly enhancing my programming skills.

Haskell, Python & Java again

My university studies reintroduced Java and introduced me to Python, a language I had long been eager to explore. Returning to Ruby on Rails marked a significant positive shift in my career. This return highlighted my deep appreciation for the framework. My current tech stack includes:

This combination has proven highly effective, and I anticipate continuing with this stack in the near future due to its efficiency and my personal affinity for these technologies.
